# GC-BAT Vignettes

**A 32-story narrative framework on technology governance failures and the path to international coordination, 2025–2032.**

Part of [Tech 4 Humanity Books](https://tml-4pm.github.io/tech4humanity-books).

## Scope

- 32 interconnected stories across 5 thematic arcs
- 9 recurring protagonists (Enneagram-based)
- Variable length: 15k (Tier 3) – 25k (Tier 1) words per story
- Target: ~640,000 words total
- Timeline: 2025–2032, culminating in international Governance Compact
- License: CC BY 4.0
