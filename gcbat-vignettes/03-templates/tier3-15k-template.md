# Tier 3 Story Template (15,000 words)

**Use for:** Spotlight stories. Focused, cinematic, character-driven. Warning flares, not standards frameworks.

**Spotlight stories (8):** #7, #12, #13, #19, #22, #24, #25, and others

## Structure

- **Part 1: The Event** (~5,000 words) — the narrative is the centerpiece, more cinematic detail, fewer parallel storylines
- **Part 2: Stakeholder Universe** (~3,000 words) — 3–4 key groups, streamlined
- **Part 3: GC-BAT System Response** (~5,000 words) — Marcus + 1 primary unit deep-dive (2,500 words) + 1 secondary (1,000 words) + brief synthesis (500)
- **Part 4: Roadmap & Futures** (~2,000 words) — 6-month plan, 5 key metrics, single-paragraph scenarios

## Differences from Tier 1/2

- The story does the heavy lifting (memorability > technicality)
- Lighter on technical appendices
- Can be drafted in 2–3 weeks; allows rapid response to emerging issues
- Ideal for media excerpts and public engagement
- One Tier 3 story should ladder up to a Tier 1 framework it illustrates
