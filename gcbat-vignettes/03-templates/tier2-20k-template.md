# Tier 2 Story Template (20,000 words)

**Use for:** Core stories. Fully-developed scenarios with solid policy analysis. Don't carry the standards-setting burden of Tier 1.

**Core stories (16):** #3, #4, #5, #8, #10, #11, #16, #17, #18, #21, #23, #26, #28, #29, #30, #31

## Structure

- **Part 1: The Event** (~3,500 words) — multi-viewpoint scenario, 2–3 parallel storylines
- **Part 2: Stakeholder Universe** (~5,000 words) — 4–5 stakeholder groups (drop one vs Tier 1)
- **Part 3: GC-BAT System Response** (~7,500 words) — Marcus + 2–3 primary units (1,700–2,000 words each), 1 secondary (800–1,000)
- **Part 4: Roadmap & Futures** (~4,000 words) — 12-month plan, 2 scenarios, 10 core metrics

## Differences from Tier 1

- Narrative breathes more (Part 1 is shorter, leaves room for character)
- Stakeholder analysis is targeted, not encyclopedic
- GC-BAT response focuses on 2–3 units instead of all 4
- Roadmap is 12-month not 24-month
- One Tier 2 story should reference one Tier 1 foundation story it builds on
