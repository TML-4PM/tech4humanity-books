# Sofia Rodriguez — Youth / Climate Leader

**Enneagram:** Type 7 (The Enthusiast) 
**Heritage:** Argentine, Buenos Aires → Darwin 
**Age across arc:** 16 (Story #10) → 23 (Story #32) 
**Stories:** #10, #12, #13, #22, #25, #32

## Background

Born 2009 in Palermo, Buenos Aires. Father a small-business owner (artisanal leather goods); mother a community organiser (neighbourhood food cooperative). Childhood surrounded by entrepreneurial optimism and social solidarity. Family immigrated to Sydney 2018 after Argentine peso collapse; later to Darwin (2024) for father's work. As eldest digitally-native child she became cultural bridge — translating banking, healthcare, smartphones for her parents. Early adoption made her a native. The label becomes a curse.

## Personality in crisis

Enthusiasm is superpower and vulnerability. Sees possibilities everywhere; inspiring but exhaustible. Transforms obstacles into opportunities but pays in burnout. Youth means people dismiss her — yet her generation's stakes are highest. Her arc is learning to channel enthusiasm into sustainable activism: marathon not sprint, strategic not reactive, collective not individual.
