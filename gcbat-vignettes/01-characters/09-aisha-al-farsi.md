# Aisha Al-Farsi — International Diplomat

**Enneagram:** Type 9 (The Peacemaker) 
**Heritage:** Omani, Muscat → Canberra 
**Education:** Sciences Po Paris BA IR 2008; Columbia SIPA MA Conflict Resolution 2010 
**Stories:** #26, #27, #28, #32

## Background

Born 1986, Muscat. Career-diplomat father (Omani MFA, postings across Middle East, Africa, Asia); cultural-conflict-resolution anthropologist mother (worked with UN agencies). Childhood was nomadic — 7 countries before 18. Fluent in Arabic, English, French, Swahili; conversant in Mandarin and Spanish. UN peacekeeping 2010–2015 (South Sudan, Mali, CAR) — saw how technology can help and harm. UNDP 2015 specialising in technology governance in developing countries. Seconded to Australian DFAT 2020. By 2025, Deputy Secretary for Digital Diplomacy.

## Personality in crisis

Peacemaker sees conflict as failure of communication — if she can help everyone understand each other, resolution follows. Translates: technical to political, Western to non-Western, corporate to public good. Superpower: making everyone feel heard. Weakness: absorbs too much conflict, carries others' stress. Her role in vignettes is **connector** — appears when multiple parties need mediation, disappears once they are talking directly.
