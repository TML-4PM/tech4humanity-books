# Dr. Amara Chen — Tech Insider / Whistleblower

**Enneagram:** Type 1 (The Perfectionist) 
**Heritage:** Chinese-American, Silicon Valley 
**Education:** Stanford PhD, Computer Engineering, 2009 
**Stories:** #1, #4, #11, #17, #19, #29, #32

## Background

Born 1983 to a hardware engineer father (Intel) and a QA-lead mother (Oracle). Childhood under documented standards: red-pen corrections, three-ring binders, taxonomic stamp collections. Stanford PhD thesis: *Fail-Safe Architectures in Distributed Systems* — prescient in retrospect. Left an MIT research post in 2015 for AllConnex, believing private-sector velocity would amplify impact. By 2025: Principal Engineer for Platform Reliability, overseeing a 47-person team.

## Personality in crisis

Perfectionism becomes both asset and liability. She sees systemic flaws others miss but tortures herself over each oversight. When the Subscription Disconnect hits (Story #1), her first emotion is not anger but guilt: *How did I miss this?* Her habit of meticulous documentation means she has the audit trail proving she raised concerns eight months prior — and was overruled. That evidence becomes a weapon when she transitions from loyalist to whistleblower.
