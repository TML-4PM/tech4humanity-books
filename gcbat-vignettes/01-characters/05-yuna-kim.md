# Dr. Yuna Kim — Healthcare / Crisis Leader

**Enneagram:** Type 5 (The Investigator) 
**Heritage:** Korean, Seoul → Darwin 
**Education:** Seoul National University BSc Cognitive Science 2006; MIT PhD HCI 2011 
**Stories:** #6, #10, #16, #18, #30, #32

## Background

Born 1984, Seoul. Mathematics-professor father, medical-researcher mother. Academic pressure-cooker; rebelled by choosing cognitive science over traditional medicine. MIT thesis: *Cognitive Load and Error Rates in High-Stress Medical Interfaces* — pioneering work on how bad software design kills patients. Postdoc at Mass General studying ICU interface failures. Recruited to Darwin Hospital 2013 as Director of Clinical Informatics. By 2025, Chief Digital Health Officer for Northern Territory, overseeing clinical tech for the most remote healthcare system on Earth.

## Personality in crisis

Investigator nature requires understanding root cause before acting — asset (avoids wrong solutions) and liability (sometimes action is needed NOW). Her PhD trained her to see systems; her hospital work forces her to see people. Story #6 transforms her from detached researcher into present caregiver — learning to act without complete information.

## Arc summary

- **#6 Healthcare Network Failure** — 8 hours of pre-1970s medicine across 17 patients. 5 saved. 1 dies (Mr. Richardson, drug interaction the offline digital system would have caught). She cries for four minutes in a supply closet, then returns to work.
- **#10 Attention Economy Burnout** — Diagnoses Miguel Santos. Publishes Lancet Digital Health paper *Cognitive Exploitation in Digital Platforms*. International expert on tech-induced mental health.
- **#16 Predictive Policing Gone Wrong** — Personally flagged by security algorithm. Cleared but traumatised. Testifies on physiological stress signatures of algorithmic dehumanisation.
- **#18 AI-Driven Discrimination** — Expert witness for Miguel's housing lawsuit. Begins advocating for Health Impact Assessments on AI systems.
- **#30 Emergency Response Breakdown** — Chief Medical Officer under PM Priya Sharma. 72-hour operational command. 47 lives saved by improvised logistics; 19 lost.
- **#32 The Governance Compact** — Chairs Human Wellbeing Standards working group. Health Impact Assessment Framework becomes international requirement.
