# Priya Sharma — Government Official / Political Rise

**Enneagram:** Type 3 (The Achiever) 
**Heritage:** Indian, Mumbai → Sydney/Canberra 
**Education:** LSE First Class Honours, International Relations + Economics, 2008 
**Stories:** #1 (cameo), #3, #8, #14, #26, #28, #30, #32

## Background

Born 1986, Mumbai. Father a Tata Group corporate lawyer; mother a Breach Candy Hospital surgeon; sister a Goldman Sachs investment banker. Family motto: *Excellence is expected*. Top 0.1% in Maharashtra HSC 2004. UN internship during the 2008 financial crisis — saw governments scrambling without systems. Moved to Australia 2010 (skilled migration), citizen 2015. Career: Treasury policy analyst, Industry senior advisor, Shadow Minister Chief of Staff, MP for Sydney electorate 2020. Youngest Cabinet member at 34. By 2025, Shadow Minister for Technology and Infrastructure.

## Personality in crisis

Achiever drive requires wins. Tech crises are both political opportunity and genuine threat — her ambition and her belief that systems must work are equally motivating. Her pragmatism reads as coldness; it is strategic empathy. Her HR background gives her unique lens: tech failures are organisational failures.
