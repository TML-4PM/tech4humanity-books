# Miguel Santos — Citizen / User Advocate

**Enneagram:** Type 2 (The Helper) 
**Heritage:** Catalan, Barcelona → Sydney 
**Education:** Dual degrees in Psychology and Communications, Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, 2010 
**Stories:** #1, #5, #10, #13, #18, #29, #32

## Background

Born 1988 in El Raval, Barcelona's most diverse neighbourhood. Mother a clinical trauma psychologist; father a creative director at Bassat Ogilvy. Childhood split between empathy training (active listening, perspective-taking) and persuasion craft (framing, narrative, emotional arc). 2011–2015: travelled 47 countries, lived with families in 12, documenting how different cultures build trust. Moved to Sydney 2016 on partner visa; relationship ended 2019; stayed for the city. Marketing work 2016–2024, always uncomfortable with manipulative tactics. The Subscription Disconnect (Feb 2025) is the inflection: victim becomes advocate.

## Personality in crisis

Empathy is superpower and vulnerability. He feels others' pain physically. When his smart home dies in Story #1, his first thought is not anger but *how many elderly people are trapped right now*. This drives him to action but also to burnout. By Story #10 he is hospitalised for attention-economy addiction — the same empathy that made him help others made him vulnerable to engineered engagement.
