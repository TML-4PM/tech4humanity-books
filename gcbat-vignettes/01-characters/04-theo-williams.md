# Theo Williams — Rural / Environmental Technologist

**Enneagram:** Type 4 (The Individualist) 
**Heritage:** African-American, New Orleans → Longreach, Queensland 
**Education:** Yale Economics 2009; INSEAD MBA 2011 
**Stories:** #1, #7, #21, #23, #24, #32

## Background

Born 1987 in Tremé, New Orleans. Jazz-saxophonist father, abstract-painter mother. Childhood saturated with bohemian creativity. Hurricane Katrina (2005, age 18) was transformative: watched systems collapse, neighbours organise, bureaucracy fail. Decided *chaos needs mathematicians*. Yale thesis on post-disaster resource allocation. Moved to Sydney 2012 for consulting work; bought a struggling cattle station near Longreach in 2015. Neighbours thought he was insane. By 2020, the station was profitable AND carbon-negative. By 2025 he is regional spokesperson for tech-enabled sustainable farming — and the Subscription Disconnect nearly destroys him.

## Personality in crisis

Sees connections others miss — makes him brilliant and frustrating. Pattern-matches across domains: *Subscription failure = supply chain fragility = climate vulnerability = financial contagion*. People think he is changing the subject; he is revealing system architecture. His individualism resists groupthink — often right, often isolated. The outback teaches humility; crises reveal his gift for unconventional solutions from unexpected parallels.
