Privacy Policy & Data Retention

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

What We Collect

When you download a Tech 4 Humanity book, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Book downloaded
  • Download timestamp
  • Source (e.g., GitHub Pages, tech4humanity.net)

How We Use Your Data

  • Send you the download link
  • Occasional updates about new Tech 4 Humanity books (max 4 per year)
  • Understand which books are most valuable to readers

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (AWS Sydney region) with encryption at rest and in transit.

Data Retention & Disposal

  • Active users: Data retained while you remain subscribed
  • Inactive users: After 12 months of no activity, your data is automatically deleted
  • Unsubscribe: Data deleted within 30 days of unsubscribing
  • Manual deletion: Contact troy@tech4humanity.net anytime for immediate deletion

Your Rights (GDPR Compliant)

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails

Contact

Questions? Email: troy@tech4humanity.net
